Dirty dishes are placed in the dishwasher.
The dishwasher is closed.
The dishwasher heats water to a hotter temperature.
The dishwasher directs water toward the dishes through jets.
The dishwasher adds detergent.
The dirty water drains out.
The dishwasher sprays more water to rinse the dishes off.
The water drains out again.
The dishwasher directs warm air toward the dishes to dry them.
The dishes can be removed.
