In order to recycle aluminum cans and other products, you should to place them in recycling bins.
The city or a private company is responsible for gathering and moving the aluminum to a recycling facility.
Once at the recycling facility, the aluminum will be sorted and cleaned.
In order to eradicate the coatings and inks from the aluminum, it is thawed.
Ingots are established from the thawed aluminum once it has been formed into large formations.
Ingots are taken to another facility and rolled out so that they could be handled more easily.
At this point, the ingots could be turned into new aluminum products.
