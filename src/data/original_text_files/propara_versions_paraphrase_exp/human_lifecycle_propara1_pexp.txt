A human baby develops in the womb of the mother.
After 9 months in the womb the baby is born.
It is an infant.
The infant is dependent on its parents for everything.
It drinks mother's milk for nourishment.
From 3-8 years old the child is in early childhood.
Adolescence is from roughly 9-18 years old.
During adolescence the child is growing rapidly and maturing sexually.
At 18 years, the child becomes an adult.
Adults can reproduce and have babies.
