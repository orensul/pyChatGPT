A sperm and egg meet inside a woman's fallopian tube.
The fertilized egg, now a zygote, travels to the uterus.
The zygote implants in the uterus.
The zygote becomes an embryo.
The embryo becomes a fetus.
The fetus grows for approximately 9 months.
The woman gives birth to a baby.
The baby grows into a child.
The child becomes an adult.
The adult mates with another adult.
