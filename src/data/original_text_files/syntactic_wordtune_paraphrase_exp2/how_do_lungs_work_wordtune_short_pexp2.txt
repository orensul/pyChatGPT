Breathing air in.
Bronchial tubes obtain air.
Lungs split air into bronchioles.
Alveoli are tiny air sacs in the bronchioles.
Small vessels nearby alveoli.
Alveoli take oxygen to blood vessels.
As blood proceeds through the body, it leaves the lungs.
CO2 is carried by blood to the lungs.
CO2 is discharged when you breathe out.
