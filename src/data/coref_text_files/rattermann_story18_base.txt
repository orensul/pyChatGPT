A cobra called Pierre was slithering through the brush when a falcon came towards the cobra.
Pierre became frightened and struck, wounding the falcon in the abdomen.
To teach Pierre a lesson, the falcon grabbed the cobra and flew away with the cobra.
Unfortunately for both of them, after both of them, were high above the earth the wounded falcon became too weak to fly.
Consequently, both of them, plummeted straight down to the earth and perished.
both of them, landed, strangely enough, in the middle of an outdoor wedding ceremony
