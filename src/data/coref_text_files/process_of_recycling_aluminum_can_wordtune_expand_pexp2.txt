In order to recycle aluminum cans and other products, you should to place aluminum cans and other products, in recycling bins.
The city or a private company is responsible for gathering and moving the aluminum to a recycling facility.
Once at the recycling facility, the aluminum will be sorted and cleaned.
In order to eradicate the coatings and inks from the aluminum, the aluminum is thawed.
Ingots are established from the thawed aluminum once the aluminum has been formed into large formations.
Ingots are taken to another facility and rolled out so that Ingots could be handled more easily.
At this point, the ingots could be turned into new aluminum products.
