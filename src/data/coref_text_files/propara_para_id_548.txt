The liver removes toxins from the blood.
Liver also cleans blood that has just been enriched with vitamins and minerals during digestion.
Liver processes the good stuff into forms that the rest of the body can use.
Waste or stuff your body doesn't need can be carried by bile back into the intestine or into the kidneys.
The liver also produces a digestive juice called bile that helps the body absorb fat into the bloodstream.
The liver also stores extra carbohydrates as glycogen for the body to use as fuel when needed.
