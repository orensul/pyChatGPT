Recycling aluminum cans and other products is easy.
The town or a private company collects and transfer aluminum for recycling.
At the central recycling facility, aluminum is sorted and cleaned.
Coatings and inks are detached from aluminum.
Ingots are large formations made from melted aluminum.
Ingots are rolled out in another facility to make Ingots easier to handle.
Now, the ingots might be used for aluminum products
